Getting Fitnotes workout data

  1. Open the Settings screen on the Fitnotes application on your phone.
  2. Under the Data section on the Settings screen, select Spreadsheet Export.
  3. On the next screen, select where you want to Export the data to. You could store in on your phone and upload it directly or upload it to Google Drive to use it on your desktop.
